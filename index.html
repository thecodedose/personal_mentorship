About Me

Hello, I am Urvashi. I like creating things. Currently I'm creating: content on The Code Dose, coding projects, cookies in my kitchen, clay keycaps for my keyboard.

I'm going to be your mentor on this journey towards career as well as personal growth. I've previously mentored in programs like Google Code-in and Outreachy.

Read more

My approach to mentorship is based on understanding your unique strengths, challenges, and goals. As your mentor, I am here to listen, inspire, and keep you accountable.

When it comes to achieving your goals, I'm all about the long game. Let's ditch the quick fixes and shortcuts and focus on what really matters: building habits and a mindset that stand the test of time. So let's get to work on creating a future filled with achievements you can be proud of!
